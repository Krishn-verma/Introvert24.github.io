Choosing the Right Components

The key to building a custom laptop on a budget is to carefully select each component based on performance, compatibility, and price. Here are some of the components I considered:

  • Processor: Opt for a mid-range processor that offers a balance between performance and cost.
  • RAM: Choose sufficient RAM for multitasking without overspending.
  • Storage: Opt for a combination of SSD and HDD to balance speed and storage capacity.
  • Display: Select a budget-friendly display with decent resolution and color accuracy.
  • Battery: Prioritize a long-lasting battery for on-the-go productivity.

Building the Laptop
